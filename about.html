Zetronica IT Fest

The beginning

Zetronica is a dynamic platform dedicated to fostering innovation, technology, and creativity. It serves as a hub for tech enthusiasts, developers, and problem-solvers, providing opportunities to explore the latest advancements in software, hardware, and digital transformation. With a vision to bridge the gap between academia and industry, Zetronica organizes workshops, hackathons, and tech fests that empower individuals to push the boundaries of innovation. Whether you're a student, professional, or technology enthusiast, Zetronica is your gateway to a world of endless possibilities in the digital era.

Our Vision

Our vision is to establish Tensor Tribe as a hub of creativity, knowledge-sharing, and leadership development. We strive to inspire students to think beyond boundaries, take on challenges, and make a positive impact through teamwork, interdisciplinary learning, and a spirit of continuous improvement

Our Mission

Tensor Tribe at Sapthagiri NPS University fosters innovation, collaboration, and growth. We empower students to explore, develop skills, and engage in impactful projects. Through teamwork and creativity, we enhance both academic and professional journeys.
